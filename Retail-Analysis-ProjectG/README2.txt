# Retail Business Performance & Profitability Analysis

This project analyzes transactional data of an online retailer to determine the most profitable items, slow-selling inventory, regional performance, and seasonal trends.

## Objectives

- Determine high-revenue product descriptions and categories
- Examine movement in inventory and identify overstock threats
- Uncover seasonal sales patterns to enhance planning
- Create an interactive dashboard for decision-making
- 

## Tools Used

- **Python (Pandas, Seaborn, Matplotlib)** – used for data cleaning and EDA
- **Tableau** – for interactive dashboard visualizations
- **Jupyter Notebook** – for exploratory scripting
- **GitHub** – to host and share the project

## Key Insights

- **UK** is the most profitable region
- Top 10 items generate more than 40% of all revenue
- Low movement + low margin items are slow-movers
- Sales are highest during **November–December**
